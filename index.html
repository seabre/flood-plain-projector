Flood Plain Projector

Use Flood Plain Projector to figure out if your fishery is in a location that is susceptible to flooding.

Fishery Sea Level

Choose the number of years from the current year to project. You will be shown the height above sea level for the chosen number of years.

Fishery Density

It can be useful to see the concentration of fisheries within an area. Click the checkbox to turn on the fishery density layer.
